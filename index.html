Class One - Getting Started with Script Analysis

✕

Before Class:

  • Complete the Pre-Quarter Assignment
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Note-taking supplies of choice

During Class:

  • Welcome to the sewing studio
  • Intro to Script Analysis

After Class:

  • Re-read the script at least twice more and come to class with initial costuming ideas and questions.

Class Two - Analyzing the Script

✕

Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Note-taking supplies of choice

During Class:

  • Introduction to different types of script analysis.
  • Develop a simplified action analysis for our short script.

After Class:

  • Complete and submit the Short Script Analysis via Blackboard > Submissions by the end of Unit One.
  • Begin researching costume ideas for our characters.
  • Bring your research and costume sketches to our new class meeting.
  • Prepare fabric for the bodice front and back drape.

Class Three - Designing from Analysis

✕

Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Traditional drawing supplies of choice
    • Consider bringing tracing paper or contact paper to duplicate sketches

During Class:

  • Choose one character from our script and develop a costume design for this character.
  • Using traditional drawing tools, begin to sketch this character.
  • Practice drawing different poses for your chosen character.
  • Begin developing costume ideas for your character.
  • Practice using different traditional media.

After Class:

  • Finalize your character's costume design using traditional media.

Class Four - Designing from Analysis Continues

✕

Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Drawing supplies of choice (traditional or digital)

During Class:

  • Continue developing costume designs for 2 more of the script's characters.
  • Practice drawing different poses for each character.
  • Practice using digital media and software.

After Class:

  • Complete one costume design for a minimum of three characters based on your script analysis.
  • Each costume design must feature the front and back of the costume.
  • One of the three designs must be completed using traditional media.
  • The remaining designs may be rendered with either traditional or digital media.
  • Complete and submit the Short Script Costume Designs via Blackboard > Submissions by the end of Unit Two.
  • Submit any desired presentation slides to Blackboard > Discussion Forums before our next class meeting.

Class Six - Taking Measurements

✕

Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:

✕
